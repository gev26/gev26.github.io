About me

I am a first-year EECS PhD student at UC Berkeley, broadly interested in AI-psychology, human-AI interaction, agentic systems, and AI for education. I am happily advised by Narges Norouzi and Joey Gonzalez. I am affiliated with Sky Computing Lab and Berkeley AI Research (BAIR). I am also fortunate enough to closely work with Gireeja Ranade.

Previously, I earned my B.A. degree in CS (Honors) and Statistics from the College of Computing, Data Science, and Society (CDSS) at UC Berkeley; my undergraduate research was focused on AI for Education.
